About Poa Safaris

Your trusted partner for authentic Kenyan adventures and reliable transport.

Who We Are

Based in the vibrant coastal city of Mombasa, Poa Safaris is a premier tour operator and car hire service dedicated to showcasing the true beauty of Kenya. We specialize in custom, private safaris that take you off the beaten path and into the heart of the wild.

Our Mission

To provide unforgettable, seamless, and affordable travel experiences while promoting sustainable tourism that benefits local communities and preserves Kenya's wildlife heritage.

Why Choose Us?

  • Local Expertise: Our guides know the parks intimately.
  • Custom Itineraries: We build the trip around your budget and timeline.
  • Reliable Fleet: Well-maintained vehicles for both safaris and city hire.
